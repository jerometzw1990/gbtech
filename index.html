How we work

A delivery loop, not a waterfall

Six stages we run in tight iterations, so you see working software early and decisions stay reversible.

Step 01

Discovery

We map your goals, constraints and the riskiest assumptions worth testing first.

Step 02

Architecture

System design, chain and model choices, and a build plan you can sign off on.

Step 03

Prototype

Step 04

Build & Audit

Launch
